Fourth Wing

Author: Rebecca Yarros

Genre: Fantasy, Romance

Rating: 5/5 stars

Review

Where do I even start? I was not expecting to bawl my eyes out while reading this book but oh well. This was one of the best fantasy books I've ever read. The relationship that was formed between Violet and her dragons was so sweet and funny. Tairn's sas was so hilarious, such a contrast to his otherwise cold demeanor.
